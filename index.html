Our Story

About PK Krushi Farm House

हम गुजरात के डांटीवाड़ा से किसान हैं। 1965 से हम स्मार्ट और टिकाऊ खेती को बढ़ावा दे रहे हैं।

Our mission is to bring the next generation closer to farming and advance it with modern technology — while staying true to our roots and the land we love.

We believe that किसान बिना अन्न नहीं — without farmers, there is no food. Our farm is a living testament to sustainable, organic, and joyful farming.

Our Services

From agro-tourism experiences to organic produce — discover what makes PK Krushi Farm unique.

🌾

Agro Tourism

Experience authentic farm life — sow seeds, harvest crops, and connect with nature in a truly immersive way.

🥬

Organic Produce

Fresh, pesticide-free vegetables and fruits grown with love and traditional knowledge passed through generations.

🌳

Nature Walks

Guided tours through our lush farmlands, orchards, and natural landscapes — perfect for families and students.

📸

Farm Photoshoots

Stunning backdrops of green fields and rustic farmscapes for pre-wedding, family, and creative photography.

🏡

Farm Stay

Enjoy an overnight stay surrounded by nature, fresh air, and the calming rhythm of rural Gujarat.

🎓

Agricultural Training

Workshops and hands-on training sessions on modern sustainable farming techniques for youth and students.
